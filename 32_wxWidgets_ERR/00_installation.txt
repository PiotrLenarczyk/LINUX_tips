Linking and Compiling

Normally when you include wxWidgets you say "wx/wx.h" however wxwidgets is in the subdirectory of wx-2.8/wx so what we are going to do is create a link to wx-2.8/wx. thats all now your files should compile correctly
	#sudo ln -sv wx-2.8/wx wx
When you compile your files to include the wxWidgets libraries, add this to the end of your gcc command:
	`wx-config --cxxflags` `wx-config --libs`
